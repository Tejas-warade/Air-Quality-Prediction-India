About Us

We are a dedicated team of individuals with a shared passion for environmental health and data-driven solutions. Our project revolves around predicting the Air Quality Index (AQI), a crucial metric that affects the well-being of communities worldwide.Our diverse team comprises experts in data science, environmental science, and machine learning, allowing us to tackle air quality challenges comprehensively.

Our mission is to empower individuals, policymakers, and communities with actionable insights derived from data. We firmly believe that access to clean air is a fundamental human right, and we are committed to using our expertise to make a positive impact. Through rigorous data analysis and machine learning, we have developed a robust model for AQI prediction.

Join us in our journey towards a cleaner, healthier world. Together, we can breathe life into this vision and ensure a better future for generations to come.
